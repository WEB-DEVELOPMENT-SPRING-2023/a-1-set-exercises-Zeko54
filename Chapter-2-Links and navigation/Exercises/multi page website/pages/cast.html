Cast

Jamie Foxx

Jamie Foxx is an accomplished actor, singer, and comedian who has been in the entertainment industry for over two decades. He has been praised for his versatility and range, and has appeared in a wide range of films and television shows. In "Django Unchained," Foxx delivers a powerful and emotional performance as Django, capturing the character's strength, determination, and vulnerability.

Christoph Waltz

Waltz is a talented and versatile actor who has appeared in numerous films and television shows throughout his career. He is perhaps best known for his portrayal of Dr. King Schultz in "Django Unchained," which won him the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor. Waltz's performance in the film is widely considered to be one of the highlights of the film, and he has been praised for his wit, intelligence, and sophistication.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is one of the most popular and talented actors of his generation. He has received numerous awards and nominations for his acting, including several Academy Award nominations. In "Django Unchained," DiCaprio plays Calvin Candie, the main antagonist of the film and the owner of the plantation where Django's wife is being held. DiCaprio's performance as Candie is both convincing and chilling, and he captures the character's malevolence and sadism with ease.
