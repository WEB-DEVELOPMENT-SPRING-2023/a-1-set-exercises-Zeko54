Django Unchained

Life liberty and the pursuit of vengeance

Key information

Director: Quentin Tarantino
Writer: Quentin Tarantino
Release date: December 25, 2012 (United States)
Running time: 165 minutes
Budget: $100 million
Box office: $425.4 million
Cast: Jamie Foxx , Christoph Waltz , Leonardo DiCaprio , Kerry Washington , Samuel L. Jackson
Awards: "Django Unchained" won two Academy Awards in 2013, for Best Original Screenplay (Quentin Tarantino) and Best Supporting Actor (Christoph Waltz).
