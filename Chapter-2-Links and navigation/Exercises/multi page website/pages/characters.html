Life liberty and the pursuit of vengeance

Characters

Django

Django played by Jamie Foxx is the main character of the film and a former slave who has been freed by Dr. King Schultz. Django is determined to rescue his wife from slavery, and is willing to do whatever it takes to get her back. Throughout the film, Django transforms from a vulnerable and timid man into a confident and skilled bounty hunter.

Dr. King Schultz

Dr. King Schultz played by Christopher Waltz is a German dentist turned bounty hunter who takes Django under his wing and trains him in the art of hunting outlaws. Schultz is a charming and sophisticated man who uses his wit and intelligence to get what he wants. Despite his ruthless nature, he is also shown to have a sense of justice and a strong moral code.

Calvin Candie

Calvin Candie played by Leonardo Di DiCaprio is the main antagonist of the film and the owner of the plantation where Django's wife is being held. Candie is a cruel and sadistic man who takes pleasure in causing others to suffer. He is also depicted as being intelligent and cunning, and he is able to maintain control over his plantation through fear and intimidation.
